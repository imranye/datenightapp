Privacy Policy for DateNight App

Last Updated: 3/18/2025

DateNight App ("DateNight", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

What information do we collect?

We collect minimal personal information to make the App work:

  • We use RevenueCat to manage your subscription. RevenueCat collects and stores your subscription information on our behalf. Please see RevenueCat's privacy policy for more information.
  • We do not collect any personal information unless you email us feedback, in which case we will use your email address only to respond to your feedback.

How do we use your information?

We use your information to:

  • Manage your subscription and provide you access to the App.
  • Respond to your feedback and improve the App.

How do we share your information?

We do not share your information with third parties, except as follows:

  • We share your subscription information with RevenueCat to manage your subscription.
  • We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us with our business operations, such as email providers. These service providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are not permitted to use your information for any other purpose.

How long do we keep your information?

We keep your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law.

What about children's privacy?

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we learn that a child under the age of 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete it as soon as possible.
